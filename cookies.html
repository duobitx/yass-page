Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  EDFS Project  ·  ESA Contract 4000148100/25/I-NS

I. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, improve performance, and provide information to site owners.

This website (edfs-site) is a static informational project page. It does not use any cookies of its own for tracking, analytics, or user sessions.

II. Cookies Used on This Site

This site does not set any first-party cookies. The only external service loaded by this page that may set or read cookies is Google Fonts, used to load the Inter and JetBrains Mono typefaces.

Source Purpose Type Retention
fonts.googleapis.com Serves web font CSS definitions (Inter, JetBrains Mono) Third-party Session / browser cache
fonts.gstatic.com Delivers web font files Third-party Up to 1 year (browser cache)

When your browser requests fonts from Google's servers, Google may process your IP address and browser information in accordance with their own privacy policy. No personal data is collected or stored by the EDFS project through this interaction.

For details on how Google processes data via Google Fonts, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

III. No Analytics or Tracking

This site does not use:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Matomo, etc.)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Session or login cookies
  • Any form of user profiling

IV. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. To prevent Google Fonts from loading, you can use a browser extension that blocks third-party font requests, or disable web fonts in your browser.

For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, refer to your browser's help documentation:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

V. Changes to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated if the site's technical setup changes. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the site following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
